<span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-103">Os controles do Visual Basic Power Packs linha e forma são um conjunto de três controles gráficos que lhe permitem desenhar linhas e formas em formulários e contêineres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-103">The Visual Basic Power Packs Line and Shape controls are a set of three graphical controls that enable you to draw lines and shapes on forms and containers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-104">O <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.LineShape>controle é usado para desenhar linhas horizontais, verticais e diagonais.</xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.LineShape></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-104">The <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.LineShape> control is used to draw horizontal, vertical, and diagonal lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-105">O <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.OvalShape>controle é usado para desenhar círculos e elipses e o <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.RectangleShape>controle é usado para desenhar retângulos e quadrados.</xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.RectangleShape> </xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.OvalShape></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-105">The <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.OvalShape> control is used to draw circles and ovals, and the <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.RectangleShape> control is used to draw rectangles and squares.</span></span>  
  
## <a name="line-and-shape-controls"></a><span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-106">Controles de linha e forma</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-106">Line and Shape Controls</span></span>  
 <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-107">Controles de linha e forma encapsulam muitos dos métodos gráficos contidos no <xref:System.Drawing>namespace.</xref:System.Drawing></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-107">Line and Shape controls encapsulate many of the graphics methods that are contained in the <xref:System.Drawing> namespace.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-108">Isso permite que você desenhar linhas e formas em uma única etapa sem ter de criar objetos gráficos, canetas e pincéis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-108">This enables you to draw lines and shapes in a single step without having to create graphics objects, pens, and brushes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-109">Técnicas de elementos gráficos complexos, como preenchimentos com gradiente podem ser feitas definindo apenas algumas propriedades.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-109">Complex graphics techniques such as gradient fills can be accomplished by just setting some properties.</span></span>  
  
 <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-110">Embora também seja possível desenhar linhas e formas usando métodos gráficos, há diversas vantagens em usar os controles de linha e forma:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-110">Although it is also possible to draw lines and shapes by using graphics methods, there are several advantages to using the Line and Shape controls:</span></span>  
  
-   <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-111">Métodos gráficos podem ser chamados somente em tempo de execução.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-111">Graphics methods can be called only at run time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-112">Controles de linha e forma podem ser adicionados a um formulário em tempo de design.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-112">Line and Shape controls can be added to a form at design time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-113">Isso permite que você veja sua aparência e posicioná-los; Eles também podem ser adicionados em tempo de execução.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-113">This enables you to see what they look like and to position them exactly; they can also be added at run time.</span></span>  
  
-   <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-114">Controles de linha e forma podem ser selecionados em tempo de execução, fornecendo eventos, como <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.Click>e <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.OnDoubleClick%2A>.</xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.OnDoubleClick%2A> </xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.Click></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-114">Line and Shape controls are selectable at run time, providing events such as <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.Click> and <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.OnDoubleClick%2A>.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-115">As saídas de métodos gráficos não podem ser selecionadas e não fornecer eventos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-115">The outputs of graphics methods are not selectable and do not provide events.</span></span>  
  
-   <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-116">Controles de linha e forma fornecem <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.BringToFront%2A>e <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.SendToBack%2A>métodos que permitem que você controle a ordem z em tempo de design e tempo de execução.</xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.SendToBack%2A> </xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.BringToFront%2A></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-116">Line and Shape controls provide <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.BringToFront%2A> and <xref:Microsoft.VisualBasic.PowerPacks.Shape.SendToBack%2A> methods that enable you to control their z-order at design time and at run time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-117">A ordem z de métodos gráficos pode ser controlada apenas alterando a ordem de execução em tempo de execução.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-117">The z-order of graphics methods can be controlled only by changing their order of execution at run time.</span></span>  
  
-   <span data-ttu-id="b5ce7-118">Controles de linha e forma são controles sem janelas. eles têm nenhum identificador de janela e, portanto, usar menos recursos do sistema.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5ce7-118">Line and Shape controls are windowless controls; they have no window handles and therefore use less system resources.</span></span>
