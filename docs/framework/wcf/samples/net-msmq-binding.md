 [Associação transacionada do MSMQ](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/transacted-msmq-binding.md)

 [Gerador de produto de ReceiveContext](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/receivecontext-enabled-wcf-channels.md)
