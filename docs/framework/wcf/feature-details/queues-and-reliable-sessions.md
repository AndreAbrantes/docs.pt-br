 <span data-ttu-id="ec8ce-122">As filas no [!INCLUDE[indigo2](../../../../includes/indigo2-md.md)] fornecer ambas as transferências confiáveis de mensagens e separação entre fontes e destinos às custas de alta latência.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ec8ce-122">Queues in [!INCLUDE[indigo2](../../../../includes/indigo2-md.md)] provide both reliable transfers of messages and separation between sources and destinations at the cost of high latency.</span></span> [!INCLUDE[indigo2](../../../../includes/indigo2-md.md)]<span data-ttu-id="ec8ce-123">comunicação em fila é criada sobre enfileiramento de mensagens (também conhecido como MSMQ).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ec8ce-123"> queued communication is built on top of Message Queuing (also known as MSMQ).</span></span>
